Requirements & Materials

Training

01

Initial Credentialing

All new chaplains must complete the TPD credentialing process including background check, interview, and orientation before riding.

Required
02

Ride-Along Requirement

A minimum number of supervised ride-alongs with a senior chaplain must be completed prior to independent deployment.

Required
03

Annual Recertification

Chaplains must complete annual recertification including updated training hours and a review of policy and procedures.

Annual
04

Crisis Intervention Training

CIT training is strongly encouraged for all chaplains to enhance skills in mental health crisis response alongside officers.
